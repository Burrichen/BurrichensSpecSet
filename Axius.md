Each regions unqiue resource has was created via Axius. 

In essensce, it contains the magic of thoughts, brainwaves and dreams, become tangible.
It is unclear why these particular concepts became a tangible reality in comparison to others.

Ordon - Progress. This formed a mallable, workable ore. Froms a drug that makes people hyper productive. When condensed, turns into a resource more powerful than coal. When treated lightly, it is hyper-conductive, allowing creation of steam machinery. The hardest armours are made of it, but, unknown to the public - those who wear it often find themselves unable to take it off.

Halingo - Greed. Ichor, like oil. Axius breaks up into a liquid. It contains great power when harnessed, but at a significant risk. Almost all technology created with Ichor is questionable. The great railway network, connecting the giant desert is stained black by its expenditure. It is unworkable in use of medicine, useless for power, but greatly useful in allowing the Disel Barons keep their rule with Ichor weaponry and firepower.

Skugurr - The unknown. It's not that Skgurr's is 'unknown' but that is in fact the unknown. The corruption of one of the most human resources available turned into something that is clearly defined as 'not water'. The way magic cannot interact with it whatsoever, causing unpredictable and dangerous side effects. The way it changes you, or you will need to change to weather it's existence. 

Shinkira - Change. Shinkira's element, a crystal - the epiotme of change. Unique in that it wasn't as prominent of a concept until the Oblate. But the second after it occured, change embodied the whole world - and thusly Change became one of the larger concepts. Crystalline forms, so volatile and tricky to define. When met with nature, it acts as it supposed to, evolving, a beautiful harmonisation. But when met with humans, the transition is not as easy. Some die, and some gain powerful stands, visions of one's true potential, unburdened by mortal constraints.
